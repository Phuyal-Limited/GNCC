Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Our Mission

The mission of the Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is to foster inclusive, sustainable, and globally connected business ecosystems that support Nepal’s economic engagement with international markets.

GNCC seeks to serve as a neutral, member-driven platform that enables collaboration among entrepreneurs, investors, institutions, and diaspora stakeholders, encouraging responsible trade, investment, innovation, and knowledge exchange.

Our Vision

GNCC envisions Nepal as a trusted and competitive participant in the global economy, connected through transparent partnerships, resilient enterprises, and long-term value creation across borders.

Guiding Principles

  • Independence: GNCC operates as an independent and non-political platform.
  • Inclusivity: Open engagement across sectors, geographies, and communities.
  • Collaboration: Complementing existing chambers and institutions.
  • Integrity: Commitment to transparency, ethical conduct, and good governance.
  • Global Perspective: Aligning Nepal’s opportunities with international best practices.
