Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Our Mission

The mission of the Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is to advance Nepal’s global trade competitiveness by fostering inclusive, sustainable, and globally connected business ecosystems that enable meaningful engagement with international markets.

GNCC serves as a neutral, member-driven platform that convenes entrepreneurs, investors, institutions, and diaspora stakeholders through structured dialogue, coordination, and cross-border collaboration, encouraging responsible trade, investment, innovation, and strategic knowledge exchange.

Our Vision

GNCC envisions Nepal as a trusted and influential partner in regional and global commerce, connected through transparent partnerships, resilient enterprises, and long-term, shared value creation across borders.

Guiding Principles

  • Collaboration: Building partnerships that drive shared and sustainable prosperity.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical conduct, transparency, and accountability.
  • Inclusivity: Engaging across sectors, geographies, and diverse communities.
  • Independence: Operating free from political alignment or partisan influence.
  • Global Perspective: Aligning Nepal’s opportunities with international best practices.
