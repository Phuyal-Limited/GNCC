Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Our Mission

The mission of the Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is to advance Nepal’s global trade competitiveness by fostering inclusive, sustainable, and globally connected business ecosystems that support meaningful engagement with international markets.

GNCC serves as a neutral, member-driven platform enabling collaboration among entrepreneurs, investors, institutions, and diaspora stakeholders — encouraging responsible trade, investment, innovation, and strategic knowledge exchange.

Our Vision

GNCC envisions Nepal as a trusted and influential partner in regional and global commerce, connected through transparent partnerships, resilient enterprises, and long-term value creation across borders.

Guiding Principles

Collaboration

Strengthening partnerships that drive shared prosperity.

Integrity

Upholding ethical conduct and transparent operations.

Inclusivity

Embracing diverse sectors, geographies, and perspectives.

Independence

Operating free of political alignment or bias.

Global Perspective

Adopting international best practices for sustainable impact.
