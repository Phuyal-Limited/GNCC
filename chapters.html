Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Global Chapters

The Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) operates through a chapter-based model designed to support local engagement while maintaining a shared global mission, governance framework, and institutional standards.

GNCC chapters provide structured platforms for members to collaborate, exchange insights, and advance Nepal-related economic engagement within their respective regions.

Chapter Framework

Chapters are established in accordance with GNCC’s governance principles and operate in alignment with the chamber’s mission, code of conduct, and non-political stance.

  • Chapters reflect regional priorities while contributing to GNCC’s global objectives.
  • Chapters operate with defined coordination mechanisms with the GNCC Secretariat.
  • Chapters do not function as independent legal entities unless separately incorporated in accordance with applicable local laws.

Founding & Emerging Chapters

GNCC is in the process of establishing founding and emerging chapters in key global regions where there is significant interest in Nepal-related trade, investment, and collaboration.

  • United Kingdom — Founding Chapter (in formation)
  • Ireland — Emerging Chapter
  • European Union — Emerging Regional Chapter
  • Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) — Emerging Regional Chapter
  • Asia-Pacific — Emerging Regional Chapter

Additional chapters may be established as GNCC expands its global membership and engagement footprint.

Chapter Participation

Members may participate in chapter activities based on geographic location, professional affiliation, or areas of interest, subject to GNCC’s governance framework.

Expressions of interest for chapter formation or participation may be directed to the GNCC Secretariat.
