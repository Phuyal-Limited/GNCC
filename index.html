Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce

Mobilising Global Trade, Capital & Influence for Nepal

The Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is a diaspora-led, independent global institution mobilising business leaders, investors, enterprises, and institutions to execute cross-border trade, investment, and innovation linked to Nepal.

Become a Founding Member Partner with GNCC

Why GNCC

Nepal’s engagement with the global economy has outgrown bilateral chambers and fragmented networks.

GNCC exists to replace fragmentation with coordination — enabling multi-market access, structured capital mobilisation, and institutional collaboration through a single global platform.

We operate independently, non-politically, and with a clear mandate: delivery over representation.

What GNCC Executes

Trade & Investment

Structured market access, investor engagement, and cross-border partnerships delivered through curated delegations, sector-specific roundtables, and execution-focused initiatives linked to Nepal.

Diaspora Leadership

Conversion of global Nepali expertise and capital into outcomes through leadership councils, chapter governance, and disciplined participation — not informal networking.

Digital Economy & Innovation

Positioning Nepal within global value chains by advancing technology services, digital trade, and innovation partnerships across AI, cloud, and knowledge exports.

Policy Engagement

Practical, solution-oriented engagement with governments and institutions to strengthen trade, investment, and digital-economy frameworks.

Who GNCC Is Built For

  • Enterprises seeking structured, credible global expansion linked to Nepal
  • Investors requiring trusted, de-risked access to cross-border opportunities
  • Diaspora leaders prepared to contribute capital, expertise, and governance
  • Institutions engaging with Nepal’s international economic agenda

GNCC is not designed for passive membership.

Global Structure

  • Initial international chapters
  • Diaspora-led leadership councils
  • Strategic partnerships across regions

This structure enables coordination, accountability, and continuity, rather than episodic engagement.
