Connecting Nepal to the World — and the World to Nepal

Welcome to GNCC

The Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is an independent, international, member-driven platform that brings together entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions from Nepal and across the world.

GNCC serves as a neutral forum to foster dialogue, collaboration, and responsible economic engagement with Nepal, supporting trade, investment, innovation, and diaspora-led partnerships.

Operating on global best-practice principles, GNCC aims to complement existing bilateral and sector-specific chambers by providing a cross-border, globally oriented perspective on Nepal’s economic integration and long-term growth.
