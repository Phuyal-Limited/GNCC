Connecting Nepal to the World — and the World to Nepal

Welcome to GNCC

The Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is an independent, international, member-driven platform that convenes entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions to advance Nepal’s global economic engagement.

GNCC provides a neutral platform for dialogue and collaboration, supporting trade, investment, innovation, and diaspora-led partnerships aligned with responsible and sustainable growth.

Guided by global best-practice principles, GNCC complements existing bilateral and sector-specific chambers by offering a cross-border, globally oriented perspective on Nepal’s economic integration and long-term global competitiveness.
