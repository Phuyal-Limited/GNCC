Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Events & Programmes

GNCC convenes and supports a range of forums, roundtables, and engagement programmes designed to encourage dialogue, collaboration, and cross-border economic cooperation related to Nepal.

Activities are developed in alignment with GNCC’s mission and guiding principles, and may be delivered independently or in collaboration with partner chambers, institutions, and stakeholders.

Types of Engagement

  • Flagship Forums: High-level dialogues bringing together business leaders, policymakers, investors, and subject-matter experts to discuss priority themes relevant to Nepal’s global economic engagement.
  • Roundtables & Briefings: Smaller, focused discussions on sector-specific opportunities, challenges, and best practices.
  • Delegations & Business Missions: Curated engagements connecting members with international markets, institutions, and partners.
  • Networking & Knowledge Exchange: Informal and formal opportunities for members to build relationships, share experience, and exchange insights.

Details of upcoming and past events will be published as programmes are confirmed.
