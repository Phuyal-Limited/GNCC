Leadership

The leadership of the Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is responsible for providing strategic direction, institutional stewardship, and oversight in alignment with GNCC’s mission and governance principles.

GNCC is currently operating under an interim and founding leadership framework, established to support the formation, early development, and responsible growth of the chamber.

Founding & Interim Leadership

During the founding phase, interim leadership roles are appointed to ensure continuity, operational readiness, and adherence to GNCC’s guiding principles.

Details of interim office bearers and leadership roles will be published in due course, in line with GNCC’s governance framework and transparency commitments.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Strategic Oversight: Guiding the long-term vision, priorities, and institutional direction of GNCC.
  • Representation: Acting as custodians of GNCC’s values in external engagements with members, partners, and stakeholders.
  • Governance & Integrity: Upholding GNCC’s governance framework, ethical standards, and non-political stance.
  • Institution Building: Supporting the establishment of chapters, programmes, and sustainable operational structures.

Advisory Participation

GNCC may invite distinguished individuals from business, academia, and international institutions to contribute in an advisory capacity. Advisory participants provide guidance and insight without executive authority.

Future Leadership Evolution

As GNCC matures, leadership structures and selection mechanisms may evolve to include broader member participation, nominations, or elections, consistent with international chamber practices.
