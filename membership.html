Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC)

Membership

Membership of the Global Nepal Chamber of Commerce (GNCC) is open to businesses, professionals, institutions, and individuals who share an interest in strengthening Nepal’s global economic engagement.

GNCC operates as a member-driven platform that encourages constructive participation, cross-border collaboration, and responsible business practices aligned with international standards.

Membership Categories

  • Corporate Members: Companies and enterprises operating in, with, or in partnership with Nepal, including international firms with a strategic interest in the Nepali market.
  • Individual Members: Entrepreneurs, professionals, and diaspora leaders contributing expertise, networks, or leadership to GNCC’s objectives.
  • Institutional Members: Industry bodies, academic institutions, non-profits, and public or quasi-public organizations engaged in economic development, innovation, or trade facilitation.
  • Honorary Members: Individuals recognized for their distinguished contribution to Nepal’s economic, commercial, or international engagement.

Member Participation

Members may participate in GNCC activities, working groups, events, and consultations in accordance with the chamber’s governance framework and codes of conduct.

Membership does not confer political affiliation or represent endorsement of any individual, organization, or policy position.
